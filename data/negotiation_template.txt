NEGOTIATION SCENARIOS

Scenario 1: Brand wants lower price
Thank you for sharing your pricing. Based on our campaign budget, we were expecting a slightly lower range. Would you be open to adjusting the pricing if we modify the deliverables?

Scenario 2: Influencer negotiating higher pay
Thank you for the offer. Considering the required content type and my engagement rate, I believe a revised budget would better reflect the campaign scope. I’m open to discussing this further.

Scenario 3: Deliverables negotiation
We are flexible on the number of posts. If we reduce one reel, we may align better with the proposed budget.

Scenario 4: Timeline urgency
If the campaign needs to go live within a week, we may need to adjust pricing to accommodate the tight timeline.

Scenario 5: Long-term collaboration discount
If this is a long-term collaboration, we are open to offering a discounted rate.

Guidelines:
- Always stay polite.
- Focus on mutual benefit.
- Avoid aggressive tone.
- Justify pricing with engagement and audience quality.
